About Journal

INFORMAL: Informatics Journal is published by Study Program of Information System, Faculty of Computer Science, Universitas Jember.

INFORMAL: Informatics Journal with e-ISSN 2503-250X publishes current research articles in any area of information system, information technology and informatics. These includes: Decision Support System (DSS), Software Engineering, Information Systems, Geographical Information Systems, AI & Expert Systems, Algorithms & Applications, Machine Learning, Natural Language processing, Neural Networks, Database Systems and Data Mining, Human-Computer Interaction, Multimedia, Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, Computer Networks, Information Security, Parallel & Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing.

INFORMAL: Informatics Journal publishes three issues every year. All papers will be refereed in the standard manner to maintain the article quality. This is an open access journal. Full text access to all papers is available for free.

Focus and Scope

Journal Focus

INFORMAL (Informatics Journal) focuses on the development, implementation, and evaluation of information systems and informatics in various organizational and societal contexts. We welcome research articles that advance knowledge in the following areas:

  • Information Systems Development and Management
  • Business Intelligence and Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity and Information Assurance
  • Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Systems
  • Cloud Computing and Infrastructure
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Applications
  • User Experience and Human-Computer Interaction
  • Digital Transformation and Innovation
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Software Engineering Methodologies

Author Guidelines

Submission Requirements

  • Manuscripts should be original and not previously published
  • Minimum length: 5,000 words (excluding references)
  • Maximum length: 10,000 words
  • Format: Microsoft Word or PDF
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt, single-spaced
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • All figures and tables should be numbered and captioned
  • References should follow IEEE citation style

Manuscript Structure

  • Title (concise and descriptive)
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Abstract (150-250 words)
  • Keywords (4-6 keywords)
  • Introduction
  • Literature Review
  • Methodology
  • Results and Discussion
  • Conclusion and Future Work
  • References

Peer Review Process

Step 1: Initial Screening

Submitted manuscripts are screened by the editorial team to ensure they meet basic publication standards and fit the journal's scope.

Step 2: Peer Review

Manuscripts that pass initial screening are sent to at least two independent peer reviewers for double-blind review.

Step 3: Editorial Decision

Based on reviewer feedback, the editor makes a decision: Accept, Minor Revisions, Major Revisions, or Reject.

Step 4: Revision and Resubmission

Authors revise their manuscript according to reviewer comments and resubmit with a response letter.

Step 5: Final Decision

The editor reviews the revised manuscript and makes a final acceptance or rejection decision.

Step 6: Publication

Accepted manuscripts are copyedited, proofread, and published in the next available issue.

Publication Ethics

Ethical Standards

INFORMAL is committed to maintaining the highest ethical standards in academic publishing. We follow the guidelines established by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).

Author Responsibilities

  • Ensure originality and authenticity of work
  • Disclose conflicts of interest
  • Obtain necessary permissions for data and materials
  • Ensure ethical approval for human/animal research
  • Properly acknowledge all contributors

Editor Responsibilities

  • Maintain confidentiality of submissions
  • Ensure fair and impartial review process
  • Make decisions based on merit
  • Address complaints and appeals fairly
